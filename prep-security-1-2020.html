Cyber Security and Ethical Hacking

Our main focus this week will be on learning about cyber security and ethical hacking. We also talk briefly about the need for cybersecurity analysts and why a career in this domain is much in demand.

Learning Goals for this Week

By the end of this week, you will:

  • Learn about cyber security.
  • Understand what ethical hacking is and why it is needed.

And by completing the Lab, you will:

  • Get familiar with Virtualization, VirtualBox and Kali Linux.
  • Install Kali Linux in Virtualbox.
